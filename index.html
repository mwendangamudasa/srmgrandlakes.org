Who We Are

A Closed Network for Those Who Operate in Complexity

The Security Risk Monitoring (SRM) Network — Grand Lakes & Africa is a closed, vetted professional network operating at the intersection of security, humanitarian action, diplomacy, and policy across the Great Lakes region and the African continent.

We bring together experienced professionals who share a commitment to rigorous analysis, operational integrity, and strategic intelligence sharing in some of the world's most complex environments.

Membership is by application only. Every member is individually vetted. Access is granted — not assumed.

Who Belongs in This Network

The SRM Network is designed for professionals with demonstrated experience in complex, high-stakes environments across Africa and the Great Lakes region.

🛡
Security Professionals

Security managers, risk analysts, field security officers, and crisis response specialists operating in fragile and conflict-affected states.

🌍
Humanitarian Actors

NGO directors, humanitarian coordinators, and protection specialists with operational experience in complex emergencies across the region.

🏛
Diplomatic & Policy

Diplomats, embassy staff, policy advisors, and government liaisons engaged with African peace and security architecture.

🔬
Researchers & Analysts

Conflict researchers, intelligence analysts, and think tank professionals with substantive expertise on the Great Lakes and African security dynamics.

⚖
Legal & Compliance

International law practitioners, compliance officers, and human rights monitors operating within African institutional and legal frameworks.

🤝
Civil Society Leaders

Community leaders, peacebuilding practitioners, and civil society representatives with frontline engagement in conflict-affected communities.

"Intelligence shared among trusted professionals saves lives and shapes better outcomes."
Join the Network

Closed & Vetted

Every member undergoes individual review. Membership is not open — it is earned through professional standing, demonstrated expertise, and commitment to the network's principles.

Confidential by Design

All members sign a binding Code of Conduct and Confidentiality Agreement. Information shared within the network remains within the network.

Operationally Focused

This is not a theoretical forum. The SRM Network serves professionals who operate in the field — providing practical intelligence, peer support, and strategic analysis that directly informs decision-making.

Regionally Rooted

Deep focus on the Great Lakes region and the African continent — the complexity, the actors, the dynamics. This is not a generic security network. It is purpose-built for this geography.
